  • What is the Hub?

    Once completed The Crowd Found Hub will be a community resource that provides the necessary tools to allow distributed groups, made up of individuals who are operating voluntarily, to collaborate in a transparent and sustainable way on inspiring projects, while being free from hierarchical control structures. 

    It will allow for each person to be rewarded for their contributions while protecting the individual from ruinous losses in time, energy and finances due to the high degree of risk often associated with investing in new endeavors. Success of projects developed using the Hub will be dependent on many factors, not least of which will be the understanding and appropriate use of the tools it makes available to participants.

  • Core Principles & Philosophy

    Incentivise contributors and preserve value needed to develop the Crowd Found Hub ecosystem throughout the bootstrapping phase and future projects developed on the Hub.

    The necessity to incrementally build trust and alignment between individuals who, while working together, are yet continuing to labor in freedom.

    Hedging against investor loss to ensure that real value is being built into the project as investor resources are used

    Benefit the decentralized ecosystem as a whole.

    The necessity to fairly track and reward real value contribution (financial as well as creative) which is sustainable.

    Provide an experience in Agile Development successes (especially in the software field, but very broadly applicable).

    Clearly define and algorithmically enforce the contracts between the Makers of Crowd Found Hub application development and the community providing the resources needed to go from theory to practice.

  • Hub Tokens (HT) Backed by Physical Gold and Work

    One of the key purposes of this funding plan is to retain value of crowdsale contributions in a liquidatable and returnable form in inverse ratio to the value added toward the final, functioning Crowd Found Hub.

    The initial bootstrapping phase for funding the Crowd Found Hub Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will be managed by the founding project members over phases that will cover many milestones. The founding members appreciate the potential value of structured data on the SAFE network, but agree the Ethereum ecosystem provides more than enough features for this phase of development, and should even be able to host the final tokenization system and smart contracts if there should be a problem with SAFE’s development. At this stage of development and funding, all parties who wish to be involved will benefit from the various autonomous functions the Ethereum platform offers. These include smart contract based crowdfunding (auto token creation, ETH API for exchanges) and decentralized open-ledger with transparency of funds, contracts and reward payouts. The values associated with this platform are in alignment with our own. They include privacy and the right to anonymity, non-exclusion, non-aggression, and mutually voluntary participation.

    The Crowd Found Hub Bank is self-governing (Locked Smart Contracts) and not influenced by outside forces: its software operates autonomously and its by-laws are immutably chiseled into the Ethereum blockchain. The Crowd Found Hub Bank has total control of the store of value that will include ETH, PoA physical gold, and DigixGold. Additionally, The Crowd Found Hub Bank is the only Minter of Hub Tokens (HT) after the completion of the crowdsale. Hub Tokens are the only way value (DigixGold) can flow out of The Crowd Found Hub Bank, thus the individual/group/entity with the largest share of risk also has the most assurance of stored value. This also means Hub Tokens (HT) will have an immediate inherent value because it is backed by gold.

    This bank will be untouched, except to pay storage carrying charges on its contents, from its contents. Thus this bank will retain the bulk of its value over the time of its existence and will not be subject to the high volatility and market manipulation experienced in the crypto-economy. Ergo, adding additional stability and predictability to project development.

  • Receive Hub Tokens from the Crowd Sale and have immediate value as well as huge future potential

    Typical crowdsales require capital investors to hold the majority of risk up front and at the same time this can disincentivise the builders to spend any further sweat equity towards the project because the workers received the entire amount of value up front; any expenditures cuts into received crowdsale funds and often their only source of income from the project. Workers are hesitant to join a project later because they did not receive immediate value of the crowdsale and now are dependant on wage pay (Direct cost to project) for their work provided. Plus, it is also difficult to anticipate the revenue success of a startup project and if the project becomes as successful as a company like Apple then the workers see a disproportionate return on investment (physical work) when compared to capital investors.

    Hub Tokens (HT) keep an elegant balance between risk and reward as well as the balance of power over the project of capital investors and Makers (Workers). As more work value is created for the project, more stake and DigixGold rewards will be received by the Makers (Workers). Simultaneously, the early capital investors will initially receive the largest monthly DigixGold payouts to their Ethereum wallets holding HT; as well as enjoy the possible increase in HT value as useful features from the Hub are built. If the Makers chose to trade their earned HT (Stake in future reward) for immediate capital, then the capital investor will continue to hold the majority of stake in the project and future rewards.

    The only way DigixGold can be paid out from Crowd Fund Bank is through Hub Token (HT) addresses. This incentivises Makers to complete more story points (work) to gain more stake in the project and DigixGold distributions. Initially, crowdsale backers hold the highest risk since they already provided value (ETH) before the majority of work they backed can be complete. This ensures Makers will not be able to further draw value from community provided funds until agreed work value is put towards the development of Crowd Found Hub beyond Milestone I.

Crowd Found Hub hopes to bring light into all aspects of building great ideas into reality.

At anytime the community member can see what and how much value is stored (Most in gold), transparent transactions/contracts, and also quantifiable agile story point tally to follow Maker progress.

Hub's Bootstrap Development Goals

Incentivize Contributors

Incentivise contributors and preserve value needed to develop the Crowd Found Hub ecosystem throughout the bootstrapping phase and future projects developed on the Hub.

Labor In Freedom

Allow workers to labor in freedom and backers to participate in freedom through the purchasing of Hub Tokens and their eventual use within the ecosystem.

Clearly define and Algorithmically Enforce

The contracts between the Makers of Crowd Found Hub application development and the community providing the resources needed to go from theory to practice need to have the proven transparency and security provided by Ethereum's Blockchain Smart Contracts.

Platform Independence

The concepts and tools should be portable to any platform that implements the features used by the Hub, but the value infused in the system in the form of Hub Tokens will remain with the initial iteration, but the technological backend used to support the token could change as advances in digital asset tokenization are made.

Feature Trail to MVP

Crowd Found Hub is going to build and prove its very own ecosystem with the same core values and features we use for bootstrapping our own project development.

CrowdSale

The story begins, Milestone I

This milestone marks the beginning of the Hub Founder's taking the ideas and philosophy learned from other open and crowd sourced projects and applying them to build the foundation of how the bootstrapping process will flow.

The main features created in this phase is the Crowdsale, Crowd Found Hub Bank, and Hub Maker DAO, Ethereum smart contracts. Additionally, bootstrapping considerit, discourse forum, pivotal tracker with full integration in the Hub's Main website. 

One Month After CrowdSale

The worlds first month with Hub Tokens, Milestone II

The gold backed Hub Token (HT) will be peer-to-peer trading as well as hopefully on a few public exchanges.  After the minting of new HT, ten percent (10%) of the Hub Bank's total value will flow out to every HT in existence.  This will happen every month until completion of MVP or the Hub Maker DAO fails to reset the liquidation switch every six weeks.

The HubMakerDAO will complete the first of many monthly payouts of received minted HT to the Makers, based on the number of Story Points the Makers completed.

Two Months After Crowdsale

HubMakerDAO fully operational, Milestone III

Individual profiles of Makers and an easy way for the community to track Maker's contributions will start to gain confidence. Makers will have the possibility to be individually backed financially by communities through their personal Maker Tokens.

This will also be the first milestone completed based purely on iteration and approved feature proposals by the HubMakerDAO.

Eight Months After CrowdSale

Global Domination, Milestone IX

This milestone will mark the first time Maker earned Hub Tokens outnumbers the Crowdsale minted Hub Tokens in existence.  Theoretically, if Makers keep all HT they have earned then the workers have more stake in the project (and gold in the bank) than the capital investors.

Of course Makers have the choice to trade, sell, give away their earned HT.  The more choices we all have, the more freedom we all live with.

  • Stories, Features and Ideation

    Feature Ideation
    Possible future features and stories will first start with Ideations on the Crowd Found Hub forum (http://forum.crowdfoundhub.io. The validity of these features can be ideated upon until a story consensus is reached within https://crowdfoundhub.consider.it . Makers will use a process called Estimation Poker to estimate and assign complexity of each story.

    Story Creation
    Once consensus is reached within https://crowdfoundhub.consider.it, similar or related stories will be standardized into a Feature Proposal that a Maker Badge Holder will submit to the HubMakerDAO for vetting. Founder Badge Holders are able to directly submit feature proposals to the HubMakerDAO without first doing the vetting vote. 

  • New Feature Proposal Governance by HubMakerDAO

    Valid Feature Proposals by HT Quorum then will be manually created within the Crowd Found Hub Pivotal Tracker project management tool with already designated Stories and Story Points.

  • Creation, Destruction and Rewarding of Story Point Tokens by HubMakerDAO

    HubMakerDAO creates, distributes, and can also destroy Story Point Tokens (SPT), which is governed by the Founder Badge Holders. A Story point token’s primary purpose is to keep record of work by Makers and distribute received value like newly minted HT from the Crowd Found Hub Bank. The function and/or purpose of SPT’s may change throughout Crowd Found Hub’s development process so the feature of minting and destroying needs to be unlocked and solely controlled by the Founder Badge Holders to ensure the dynamic creative process is agile.

  • Hub Token Reward Claiming

    There will be a window period where story point token holders can submit their claims for rewards to HubMakerDAO every month. Users will send their Story Point Token balance using a proxy address to identify their sending wallet 1:1. Story Point Tokens that are sent will be locked up for the duration of the claiming window to prevent double claims, before being returned together with the rewards from HubMakerDAO. Once the claim window closes, all HT that is collected by HubMakerDAO will be distributed evenly to those who submitted the claim. Hence, if a Story Point Token holder does not participate in the claiming window, the portion unclaimed will be apportioned to those who did.
