  • Crowd Found Hub

    Once completed The Crowd Found Hub will be a community resource that provides the necessary tools to allow distributed groups, made up of individuals who are operating voluntarily, to collaborate in a transparent and sustainable way on inspiring projects, while being free from hierarchical control structures. 

    It will allow for each person to be rewarded for their contributions while protecting the individual from ruinous losses in _time_, _energy_ and _finances_ due to the high degree of risk often associated with investing in new endeavors. Success of projects developed using the Hub will be dependent on many factors, not least of which will be the understanding and appropriate use of the tools it makes available to participants.

  • Project mission

    The mission of the Hub is to ***Empower the Individual to Labor in Freedom***, freedom being one of three key tenets of the SAFE Network. 

    This quote emphasises the Hub’s mission:
    “Everything that is really great and inspiring is created by the individual who can labor in freedom.” ~ Albert Einstein

  • Crowd Found Hub Decentralized Pseudo-autonomous Organization (Hub-DPO)

    The Crowd Found Hub Decentralized Pseudo-autonomous Organization (Hub-DPO) Initial (Possibly only public offering) creation phase funding for Crowd Found Hub’s Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will be managed by the founding project members over phases that will cover multiple milestones. 

    All parties involved will benefit from the various autonomous functions the Ethereum platform offers like smart contract based crowdfunding (auto token creation, ETH API for exchanges use), decentralized open-ledger with transparency of funds/contracts/reward payout. As well as DAO values that include Privacy and the right to anonymity, non-exclusion, non-aggression, voluntary participation

  • Numerous ways to Gain Value, Beyond the end product all remaining stored value is returned to the token holders.

    Inclusion: The Hub-DPO leverages smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain so that anyone, anywhere in the world can be empowered to participate in developing the platform. In exchange for their early help, participants receive Hub tokens which hold many benefits.

    Flexibility: The Hub-DPO allows for backers to purchase Hub Tokens that come into existence as Makers finish Milestones and the Makers are rewarded for work done. 

    Partially-autonomous Governance: Hub Token Holders will eventually be able to pledge towards features of the Hub beyond the MVP, as well as towards other projects within the Hub ecosystem. 

    Profitability and Growth: Each milestone will bring into existence a specific number of Hub Tokens based on a ratio of the aggregate estimation/story points. Those Hub Tokens will be divided among backers based on their percentage of the total sale amount. Each subsequent milestone will decrease the ratio of Hub Tokens to estimation story point. The project’s is planning multiple crowd funds at each feature milestone with less newly minted tokens to investors and makers at each phase; all with the goal to get the product to MVP. The development team will be receiving a percentage of the crowd sale funds and snowballing the rest forward. The full stored value amount will not be released to the token holders until the MVP achieved. The development percentage payout will decrease with each feature-milestone/sale and it is possible we will not require further funds and may not offer further tokens to the public. The snowball of stored value will likely be the largest percent of total capitalization , ergo will provide than enough incentive to achieve the MVP faster and bring in new talent (Earliest contributors will gain the most stake/tokens because of the highest risk) to the ecosystem.

Crowd Found Hub hopes to bring light into all aspects of building great applications.

At anytime the community member can see what and how much value is stored (Most in gold), transparent transactions/contracts, and also quantifiable agile story point tally to follow Maker progress.

  • Feature Proposal and Ideation

    Features will be proposed on the Crowd Found Hub forum (http://forum.crowdfoundhub.io). 

    The validity of these features can be ideated upon until a consensus is reached.

  • Story Creation

    Once consensus is reached within the forum a story or stories will be created within the Crowd Found Hub Pivotal Tracker project management tool. Makers will use a process called Estimation Poker to estimate the complexity of each story.

  • Story Completion and Hub Token Creation

    When a Story is completed the Estimation Points are assigned as Milestone Tokens to the Maker(s) who did the work to complete the Story. At the same time Hub Tokens are created at a ratio to Estimation Points. These Hub Tokens are put into a pool for a Milestone Sale Event.

  • Design of the Hub Token

    Create a tradeable digital token that can be used as a currency, a representation of an asset, a virtual share, a proof of membership and anything else we discover in development. These tokens use a standard ETH coin API, so Hub Token will be automatically compatible with any Ethereum wallet, other contract or exchange also using this standard.

    The total amount of tokens in circulation will fluctuate based on programmed ruleset.

Creation, Sale, Distribution and Eventual Usages of Hub Tokens

The processes described here are described using existing tools. We need a way to run and define our processes before the hub exists. We will learn a ton about what to include and what not to include in the Hub by creating and following a developing process workflow.

We will be making modifications to the workflow as lessons are learned. Eventually we will have a strong set of processes that can be turned into actual Hub features. Those eventual features will be tied to Smart Contracts where needed.
